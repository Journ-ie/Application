User Guide

Journal

The Journal section allows you to enter and manage your daily logs. You can view and organise both old and recent entries using labels, as well as edit or delete them. Each day features an inspirational quote to motivate your personal development. Creating a new entry is simple: click the plus icon at the top of the screen or browse through your past pictures in the memory view.

Create an Entry

When creating a new entry, you can add a title, labels, and media. Write your log in the provided text area and submit it by clicking the button at the top right.

Mood Tracker

The Mood Tracker helps you monitor your mood throughout the week. You're encouraged to log your daily mood, which will be reflected in your profile system, giving you an overview of your week's emotional trends.

Deactivate Account

If you decide to stop using Journ.ie, you can delete your account through the settings in your profile. At the bottom of the settings page, there is a button to deactivate your account. If you have any concerns or need assistance, please reach out to us via the Contact Form above.
